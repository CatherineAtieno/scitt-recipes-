Crunchy KFC Chicken Recipe

Description

This homemade KFC-style crispy fried chicken is packed with a flavorful blend of spices, coated in a crunchy batter, and deep-fried to golden perfection. Perfect for a family meal or a weekend treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken drumsticks or wings
  • wash and rinse well
  • marinate with preferred spices
  • boil in the fire for five to ten minutes
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powoder
  • 1/2 tsp dried ginger
  • 1/2 tsp of dried tumeric powoder
  • 1/2 tsp curry powoder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together buttermilk and egg. Add the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. In another bowl, mix flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, ginger, tumeric, curry, and baking powder.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan to 170°C (340°F).
  4. Take each piece of chicken from the buttermilk, coat it well in the flour mixture, and shake off excess flour.
  5. Fry in hot oil for about 10-12 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove and place on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce!
